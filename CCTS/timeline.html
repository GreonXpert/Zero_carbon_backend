📋 CCTS Regulatory Journey

Compliance Journey & Milestones

Key regulatory events from legal foundation to active compliance obligations

2022
⚖️
Legal Foundation
Legal Basis Created
Carbon trading framework enabled under the Energy Conservation (Amendment) Act, giving the Central Government statutory authority to establish India's carbon credit trading scheme.
EC Amendment Act 2022
Jun 2023
🌏
Market Created
CCTS Officially Notified
Indian Carbon Market framework formally begins. India's first statutory carbon market established under S.O. 2825(E). Compliance architecture starts taking shape.
Gazette S.O. 2825(E)
Dec 2023
🔧
Framework Strengthened
CCTS Amended
Institutional and implementation framework strengthened through S.O. 5369(E). Updated provisions and clarifications published by BEE for improved operational clarity.
Amendment S.O. 5369(E)
Oct 2025
🎯
Targets Notified
First GEI Target Rules Notified
Initial obligated sectors receive official GEI targets — S.O. 4370(E). Baseline: 2023–24. Target years: 2025–26 & 2026–27.
Aluminium Cement Chlor-Alkali Pulp & Paper CGU
Jan 2026
🔄
Scope Expanded
Amendment Rules Notified
Additional sectors brought into the notified compliance universe. More companies now require immediate readiness planning for CCTS obligations.
Secondary Aluminium Petroleum Refinery Petrochemical Textile
2026 — Now
