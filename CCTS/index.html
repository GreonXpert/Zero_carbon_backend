Official Sources & Legal References

All documents published by MoEF&CC & Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE)

Amendment Act
01
Energy Conservation (Amendment) Act, 2022
📜 Statutory Basis MoEF&CC / BEE
Created the statutory basis allowing the Central Government to specify a carbon credit trading scheme under the Energy Conservation Act.
🔗 Open Official Document
S.O. 2825(E)
02
Carbon Credit Trading Scheme (CCTS), 2023
📅 28 June 2023 Gazette Notification
Officially notified India's first statutory carbon market framework under the Energy Conservation Act 2022. Established the compliance and trading mechanism for carbon credits.
🔗 Open Official Document
S.O. 5369(E)
03
CCTS Amendment, 2023
📅 19 December 2023 Amendment Notification
BEE's official amendment to the Carbon Credit Trading Scheme, 2023. Supplements the original CCTS with updated provisions and clarifications.
🔗 Open Official Document
S.O. 4370(E)
04
Greenhouse Gases Emission Intensity Target Rules, 2025
📅 October 2025 MoEF&CC / BEE Primary Regulation
Sets compliance GEI targets for obligated entities. Baseline year: 2023–24. Target years: 2025–26 & 2026–27. Covers first set of 4 obligated sectors: Aluminium, Cement, Chlor-Alkali, and Pulp & Paper (including Cement Grinding Unit).
🔗 Open Official Document
Amendment
05
Greenhouse Gases Emission Intensity Target (Amendment) Rules, 2026
📅 January 2026 MoEF&CC / BEE Amendment
Adds a Second Schedule expanding the notified obligated-entity universe with 4 more sectors: Secondary Aluminium, Petroleum Refinery, Petrochemical, and Textile. Total sectors now: 8 sectors.
🔗 Open Official Document
ICM Portal
06
BEE Carbon Market — Indian Carbon Market (ICM) Overview
🌐 Official Web Portal BEE, Government of India
BEE's official Carbon Market page publicly describing the institutional framework, compliance mechanism, and offset mechanism under the Indian Carbon Market / CCTS structure. Covers roles of MNRE, BEE, and the carbon credit registry.
🔗 Open Official Page
ℹ️ All documents are published by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC) and Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE), Government of India. Links open official BEE portals.
