📅 CCTS Policy Timeline

2023
Carbon Credit Trading Scheme (CCTS) Notified
India's first statutory carbon market framework established under the Energy Conservation Act 2022.
2024
Obligated Sector Identification
BEE identifies pilot sectors: Aluminium, Cement, Paper & Pulp, Chlor-Alkali for GEI-based compliance.
October 2025
GEI Target Rules 2025 Gazetted
Official entity-level Greenhouse Gas Emission Intensity targets notified. Baseline: 2023–24. Target years: 2025–26 and 2026–27.
2025–26
First Compliance Year
Entities must meet notified GEI targets. Carbon credits issued for over-performance; deficit entities must purchase credits.
2026–27
Second Compliance Year
Stricter GEI targets apply. Full carbon trading market operational under CCTS framework.
